We're not models. We hate shopping. But we wouldn't mind some clothes that fit properly. Here's the deal: you take a few measurements, enter them on our site, select a color and pay. We send you the perfect T-shirt. Done.

The system is that we buy plain T-shirts in standard sizes, black, white, gray, or blue. We automatically choose one size up and generate a pattern to modify it to your size. Our tailor cuts it to size, stitches it up, and it gets sent to you by courier. Cheaper than a designer T-shirt, and it will look much better on you!

FAQ

What if my shirt doesn't fit perfectly?
You can send it back to us! The first try could be a little wonky, but it comes with a voucher for your next shirt.
Can I get my own design on it?
Not just yet. That's part of phase two, along with other clothing items and other ways of taking the pain out of looking good. By the time we're done, you're gonna be hawt, and you'll spend less time shopping than you do now!
